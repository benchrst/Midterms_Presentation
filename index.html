Decanting

partially dead buildings; occupied and vacated at the same time

"In Bonn, for example, the second and third floor rooms of high-street buildings are commonly abandoned. The rental rates of different floors are so disproportionate that entire upper levels are valued less than the footprint of the stair at the ground. The stairs are this often removed, and the spaces above abandoned." 1

"a subjective layer to a seemingly objective and rational functionalism" 1

helping the viewer to insert themselves into the space, while still grasping the technical complexity
